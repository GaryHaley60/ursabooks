Artwork

I may love to write and tell stories, but drawing and art have never been my strong point. Unless of course, you consider the lines of tall grass I leave behind every time the blind guy tries to mow. My lovely wife doesn't buy into my tale of abstract art for the neighbor's enjoyment. To cover my artistic shortfall, I have enlisted the support of two incredible new artists and an outstanding photographer. I hope you enjoy their work as much as I do.
