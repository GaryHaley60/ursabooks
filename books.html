Artwork

I may love to write and tell stories, but drawing and art have never been my strongpoint. Unless of course, you consider the lines of tall grass I leave behind every time the blind guy tries to mow. To cover my artistic shortfall, I have enlisted the support of two incredible new artists and an outstanding photographer. I hope you enjoy their work as much as I do.
