The Pilgrimage

The Green Knight’s injuries grow worse with each passing day. Can his Lady find a way to save him from himself? There is but one answer… a pilgrimage to the top of a remote peak in search of the prophet. Can they survive the perilous trek? Will the seldom-seen hermit heal the Knight’s injuries? The road to healing holds many lessons for the young heroes. Join them in their quest.
